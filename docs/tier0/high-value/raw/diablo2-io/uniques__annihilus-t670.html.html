    Be careful when seeing the
    Diablo Clone     Messages popping up on your screen. DClone will replace the closest Superunique to your location (when outside of an Act town). Meaning, if you are in Chaos Santuary trying to kill Diablo, the clone can replace one of the seal bosses. The Mini Uber bosses (
    Lilith    ,
    Uber Duriel    , and Uber Izual) are also Superuniques. So you might be out a set of keys if Dclone spawns while running Ubers since the organ piece will not drop if Dclone has replaced one of them.
