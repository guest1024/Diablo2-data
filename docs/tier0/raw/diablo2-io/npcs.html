    All Diablo 2 Resurrected NPCs

    This is a searchable, filterable, sortable database of all Diablo 2 Resurrected NPCs. It shows the latest data from Resurrected v3.1 - Reign of the Warlock (where indicated), and otherwise uses data from Diablo 2 v1.14 LoD. Filter and browse each individual townsperson and see what services they offer, where they may be found, and which quests they provide.
