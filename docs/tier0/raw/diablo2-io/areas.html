    All Diablo 2 Resurrected Areas

    This is a searchable, filterable, sortable database of all Diablo 2 Resurrected Areas. It shows the latest data from Resurrected v3.1 - Reign of the Warlock (where indicated), and otherwise uses data from Diablo 2 v1.14 LoD. You may filter by which Act they are situated in, their area level (new alvls added), which monsters can spawn in them, and where they lead from/to.
