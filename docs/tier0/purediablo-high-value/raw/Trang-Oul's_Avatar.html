      A Vampire guise

      Trang'Oul is a dragon-god, hence some of the items being named after parts of his body (wings, claws, scales). He is meant to be responsible for maintaining "the balance" of Sanctuary and tasked first his disciple Rathma, the Nephalem and then Rathma's progeny (the Necromancers) in helping him with his task.

      Lore-wise, it's unknown why the wearer turns into a Vampire, but considering it's a Necromancer Item Set, a vampire is certainly thematic.

      Diablo II Item Set

      This is the Vampire guise and full bonuses.

      Trang-Oul's Avatar is designed for a Necromancer, with a class specific Shield (Totem), Body Armor, Helmet, Belt and Gloves. This set overall gives many good bonuses.

      Patch 1.10 added a nifty bonus to wearing this full set: the character transforms into a Vampire.

      It's only available in D2X.

      Inherent Bonus information for Class Specific items can be found here.

      * For the sorceress these skills are capped at +3 as they are native to that class.

      Development

      Trang-Oul's Avatar was introduced in Patch 1.10 together with a host of other Item Sets. Significant changes to the game made some item sets less useful and others more so. Runewords became the over-all long-term gear goal for players, taking some shine away from item sets.
